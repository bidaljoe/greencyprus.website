About Green Cyprus Ltd

Founded in 2008, Green Cyprus Ltd has grown to become one of Kampala's most trusted landscaping companies. Our mission is to create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that enhance quality of life while respecting the environment.

We combine artistic vision with technical expertise to deliver customized landscaping solutions for homes, businesses, and institutions across Uganda. Our team of certified professionals is committed to excellence in every project we undertake.
